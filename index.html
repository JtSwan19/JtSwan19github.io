Basic Bundle

In this bundle, you get the basic necesities for living on your very new island. When you arrive at the airport, you will be able to choose which island to colonize with a choice of four islands. When you arrive you will be given a phone. which will contain most of what you need. On the phone, there is a recipe app, which will help you make various recipes, an outfit picker, and more! You will also get a tent, where you can place wherever you desire. Don't forget that with this bundle, you will have three other inhabitants on your island.

Expanded Bundle

With this bundle, you get everything in the previous bundle, plus even more! First, you immediately get the Miles Pass, which lets you visit other islnds and get rewards for your island. On these islands, you can bring some villagers to your own island and choose which ones you want. Lastly, you get a store on your island where you get products to help you on your adventures.

Utimate Swany Bundle

This bundle has everything in the other two bundles and more. First you get a variety of shops like bakeries, clothing stores, and more. Also, you can customize your clothes and hair with a personal stylist. You start on the island with a million of your island's currency as well. Lastly, with the sea port. you can explore the ocean and collect fish and other sea creatures.
